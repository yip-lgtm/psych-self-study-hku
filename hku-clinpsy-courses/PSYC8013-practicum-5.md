# PSYC8013 — Practicum V

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8013
- **Title:** Practicum V
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2 (typically final semester of practicum sequence before PSU)
- **HKU Description:** Fifth and final pre-PSU supervised clinical practicum. Near-entry-level competency expected. Final integration of clinical skills in preparation for PSU In-House Practicum.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC8012
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Final practicum before PSU capstone

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Demonstrate entry-level clinical competencies
2. Manage a full caseload independently
3. Apply integrated assessment and treatment
4. Engage in peer supervision
5. Plan for PSU In-House Practicum
6. Demonstrate professional autonomy
7. Develop entry-level career plan
