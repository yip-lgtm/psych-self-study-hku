# PSYC8005 — Ethics and Professional Issues

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8005
- **Title:** Ethics and professional issues
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1 or 2 (flexible)
- **HKU Description:** Ethical principles, professional standards, and legal framework governing clinical psychology practice in Hong Kong. Builds on PSYC7024 induction.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7024
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Comprehensive ethics and professional issues

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply HKPS Code of Professional Conduct
2. Apply APA Ethical Principles
3. Navigate HK legal framework
4. Address complex ethical dilemmas
5. Practice within scope of competence
6. Manage professional relationships
7. Develop professional identity

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Foundational Ethical Principles (4 hours)
- Beneficence and non-maleficence
- Fidelity and responsibility
- Integrity
- Justice
- Respect for people's rights and dignity

### Topic 2: HKPS Code of Professional Conduct (5 hours)
- Structure and content
- Application to practice
- Case examples
- Updates and revisions

### Topic 3: APA Ethical Principles (4 hours)
- Comparison with HKPS
- International applications
- Cross-cultural ethics

### Topic 4: HK Legal Framework (6 hours)
- Mental Health Ordinance (Cap 136)
- Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance
- Guardianship Board
- Advance directives and DNR
- Mandatory reporting

### Topic 5: Informed Consent (4 hours)
- Capacity assessment
- Information disclosure
- Documentation
- Special populations (children, cognitive impairment)

### Topic 6: Confidentiality (4 hours)
- Limits of confidentiality
- Tarasoff and duty to protect
- Records management
- Electronic records
- HK Privacy Commissioner

### Topic 7: Boundaries and Multiple Relationships (4 hours)
- Types of boundaries
- Boundary crossings vs. violations
- Small community considerations (HK)
- Social media
- Gift-giving

### Topic 8: Competence (3 hours)
- Scope of practice
- Continuing professional development
- Recognizing limits
- Seeking consultation

### Topic 9: Ethical Decision-Making (5 hours)
- Ethical decision-making models
- Case analysis
- Consultation
- Documentation of ethical reasoning

### Topic 10: Special Ethical Issues (5 hours)
- Telepsychology
- Social media
- Cultural considerations
- End-of-life issues
- Research ethics

### Topic 11: Professional Identity (3 hours)
- Roles and responsibilities
- Professional associations (HKPS, ICHCP)
- Public communication
- Advocacy

### Topic 12: HK-Specific Issues (3 hours)
- Working in HK Chinese context
- Cross-border practice (Mainland)
- Registration requirements (DCP)
- Setting up practice

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Beneficence | Doing good |
| Non-maleficence | Avoiding harm |
| Fidelity | Keeping promises |
| Integrity | Honesty |
| Justice | Fairness |
| Informed consent | Voluntary agreement based on understanding |
| Capacity | Decision-making ability |
| Confidentiality | Protecting client information |
| Duty to protect | Duty to warn identifiable victims |
| Boundary | Professional limit |
| Scope of practice | Areas of competence |
| Multiple relationships | More than one type of relationship |
| Telepsychology | Psychology services via technology |
