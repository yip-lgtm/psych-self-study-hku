# PSYC7024 — Induction Programme

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7024
- **Title:** Induction programme
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology (2-year training)
- **Year:** 1 (entry)
- **HKU Description:** Orientation to the clinical psychology profession, program structure, faculty, practicum sites, ethical framework, and self-care. Sets the foundation for the two-year training program.
- **Prerequisites:** Program admission
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (typically participation, reflective essays, induction activities)
- **Notes:** Entry program; orients students to training

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Describe the structure and expectations of the clinical psychology training program
2. Discuss professional identity as a clinical psychologist
3. Identify ethical and legal frameworks governing practice in HK
4. Apply self-care and resilience strategies
5. Navigate program resources (faculty, supervisors, sites)
6. Demonstrate foundational clinical skills (observation, reflection)

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Program Overview (3 hours)
- Mission and structure
- Year-by-year expectations
- Practicum sequence
- Research thesis timeline

### Topic 2: Profession of Clinical Psychology (4 hours)
- History of clinical psychology
- HK context (HKPS, DCP registration)
- Roles in different settings
- Career trajectories

### Topic 3: Ethical Framework (4 hours)
- HKPS Code of Professional Conduct
- APA Ethical Principles
- Confidentiality
- Boundaries
- Dual relationships

### Topic 4: Legal Framework (3 hours)
- Mental Health Ordinance (HK Cap 136)
- Guardianship / Voluntary admission
- Mandatory reporting
- Records and access

### Topic 5: Self-Care and Resilience (4 hours)
- Vicarious trauma
- Compassion fatigue
- Burnout prevention
- Mindfulness for clinicians
- Supervision as self-care

### Topic 6: Diversity and Cultural Humility (3 hours)
- HK multicultural context
- Working with diverse populations
- Cultural formulations in DSM-5
- Linguistic considerations (Cantonese, Mandarin, English)

### Topic 7: Foundation Clinical Skills (5 hours)
- Observation and listening
- Reflective practice
- Case formulation basics
- Professional writing

### Topic 8: Program Resources (2 hours)
- Faculty
- Library and research resources
- Practicum sites
- Support services

### Topic 9: Goal Setting (2 hours)
- Personal training goals
- Learning plan
- Self-assessment

### Topic 10: Capstone Experience Introduction (2 hours)
- Dissertation overview
- PSU In-House Practicum overview
- Integrating research and practice

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Clinical psychology | Application of psychology to assessment/treatment of mental health |
| Practicum | Supervised clinical placement |
| Competency | Specific knowledge/skill required for practice |
| Ethical framework | Principles guiding professional conduct |
| Self-care | Practices to maintain professional functioning |
| Vicarious trauma | Indirect trauma from clients |
| Cultural humility | Ongoing process of cultural self-evaluation |
| Mental Health Ordinance | HK law governing psychiatric care |
