# PSYC7005 — Psychopathology I

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7005
- **Title:** Psychopathology I
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Introduction to adult psychopathology using DSM-5-TR framework. Etiology, phenomenology, course, and treatment of major mental disorders. Emphasis on common conditions (depression, anxiety, OCD, trauma) and differential diagnosis.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7024
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (typically case vignettes, diagnostic interviews, written papers)
- **Notes:** Foundation psychopathology; DSM-5-TR framework

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply DSM-5-TR diagnostic criteria
2. Differentially diagnose common disorders
3. Discuss etiology and risk factors
4. Describe course and prognosis
5. Apply evidence-based treatments
6. Conduct diagnostic interviews
7. Discuss cultural factors

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Foundations of Psychopathology (4 hours)
- Historical concepts
- DSM-5-TR organization
- Multidimensional approach
- Cultural formulation

### Topic 2: Depressive Disorders (6 hours)
- Major depressive disorder
- Persistent depressive disorder
- Premenstrual dysphoric disorder
- Other specified depressive disorders
- Suicide risk

### Topic 3: Anxiety Disorders (8 hours)
- Generalized anxiety disorder
- Panic disorder
- Agoraphobia
- Social anxiety disorder
- Specific phobias

### Topic 4: OCD and Related (4 hours)
- Obsessive-compulsive disorder
- Body dysmorphic disorder
- Hoarding disorder
- Trichotillomania

### Topic 5: Trauma- and Stressor-Related Disorders (5 hours)
- PTSD
- Acute stress disorder
- Adjustment disorders
- Complex PTSD (proposed)

### Topic 6: Bipolar and Related (4 hours)
- Bipolar I
- Bipolar II
- Cyclothymia
- Differential with depression

### Topic 7: Sleep-Wake Disorders (3 hours)
- Insomnia
- Hypersomnolence
- Parasomnias
- Behavioral treatment

### Topic 8: Somatic Symptom and Related (3 hours)
- Somatic symptom disorder
- Illness anxiety disorder
- Conversion disorder
- Health psychology link

### Topic 9: Substance-Related (3 hours)
- Substance use disorders
- Intoxication and withdrawal
- Behavioral addictions

### Topic 10: Etiology and Risk Factors (5 hours)
- Biological (genetic, neurotransmitter)
- Psychological (cognitive, behavioral, psychodynamic)
- Social (stress, trauma, attachment)
- Diathesis-stress model
- Biopsychosocial formulation

### Topic 11: Cultural Considerations (3 hours)
- Cultural variation in expression
- Idioms of distress
- Help-seeking in HK Chinese

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Psychopathology | Study of mental disorders |
| DSM-5-TR | Diagnostic and Statistical Manual, 5th ed. text revision |
| Diagnosis | Clinical determination of disorder |
| Differential diagnosis | Distinguishing among similar conditions |
| Comorbidity | Co-occurring disorders |
| Etiology | Cause or origin |
| Risk factor | Variable increasing likelihood |
| Phenomenology | Subjective experience |
| Course | Pattern over time |
| Diathesis-stress | Predisposition + stress model |
| Biopsychosocial | Biological + psychological + social |
| Cultural formulation | Framework for cultural context |
