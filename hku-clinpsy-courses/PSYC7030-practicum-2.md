# PSYC7030 — Practicum II

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7030
- **Title:** Practicum II
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Second supervised clinical practicum. Continues Practicum I with increased complexity, autonomy, and range of cases. May be at same or different site.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7029
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Builds on Practicum I; increased complexity

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply advanced clinical skills with greater autonomy
2. Manage a more complex caseload
3. Conduct comprehensive assessments
4. Deliver evidence-based interventions independently
5. Engage in advanced supervision (including live observation)
6. Demonstrate professional development

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (3–4 hours, if new site)
- Site policies and procedures
- New systems training

### Topic 2: Complex Case Management (30–50 hours)
- Multiple presenting problems
- Comorbidities
- Treatment-resistant cases
- Severe presentations

### Topic 3: Comprehensive Assessment (15–25 hours)
- Full battery administration
- Integration of multiple data sources
- Differential diagnosis
- Report writing

### Topic 4: Intervention Skills II (30–50 hours)
- Full CBT protocols
- Other modalities (ACT, DBT, IPT) as appropriate
- Group interventions
- Crisis intervention

### Topic 5: Case Formulation II (15–20 hours)
- Advanced formulation
- Maintenance and relapse prevention
- Termination planning

### Topic 6: Supervision (weekly)
- Increased depth
- Live observation
- Co-therapy
- Greater autonomy

### Topic 7: Professional Documentation II (10–15 hours)
- Comprehensive reports
- Letter writing
- Multi-disciplinary communication

### Topic 8: Cultural Considerations (5–10 hours)
- Working with diverse populations
- Language considerations
- Cultural adaptation

### Topic 9: Consultation (5–10 hours)
- Within team
- With other professionals
- With families

### Topic 10: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 11: Case Conference Presentation (5–8 hours)
- Formal presentation
- Discussion and feedback

### Topic 12: Final Evaluation (3 hours)
