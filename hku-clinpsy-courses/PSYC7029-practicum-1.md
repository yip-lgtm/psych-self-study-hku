# PSYC7029 — Practicum I

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7029
- **Title:** Practicum I
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** First supervised clinical practicum. Students provide psychological services (assessment and/or intervention) under supervision at an approved site. Typically involves 1–2 days per week at an external site.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7024
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (supervisor evaluation, log of hours, case presentation, reflective journal)
- **Notes:** First direct clinical contact

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply basic clinical skills in a real setting
2. Conduct clinical interviews
3. Administer and score common psychological assessments
4. Deliver evidence-based interventions under supervision
5. Maintain professional documentation
6. Engage productively in supervision
7. Practice within ethical and legal frameworks

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (4–6 hours)
- Site policies and procedures
- Site-specific training
- EHR/record systems
- Emergency procedures

### Topic 2: Observation Skills (10–15 hours)
- Shadowing senior clinicians
- Observation logs
- Reflection on observed practice

### Topic 3: Clinical Interviewing (15–25 hours)
- Intake interviews
- Mental status exam
- Risk assessment
- Cultural formulation

### Topic 4: Psychological Assessment I (15–25 hours)
- Administration and scoring
- WAIS-IV, MMPI-2, etc. (as relevant to site)
- Basic interpretation

### Topic 5: Intervention Skills I (15–25 hours)
- Psychoeducation
- Basic CBT techniques
- Behavioral interventions
- Group co-facilitation

### Topic 6: Case Formulation (10–15 hours)
- Initial formulation
- Case presentation
- Treatment planning

### Topic 7: Supervision (weekly, 1–2 hours each)
- Individual supervision
- Group supervision
- Live observation
- Video review

### Topic 8: Professional Documentation (10 hours)
- Progress notes
- Reports
- Communication with referrers
- Confidentiality

### Topic 9: Log of Hours (ongoing)
- Direct service hours
- Support activities
- Supervision hours

### Topic 10: Reflective Practice (ongoing)
- Reflective journal
- Self-evaluation
- Goal setting

### Topic 11: Case Presentation (5 hours)
- Formal case presentation to faculty/peers
- Discussion and feedback

### Topic 12: Final Evaluation (3 hours)
- Supervisor evaluation
- Self-evaluation
- Goal setting for Practicum II

## Required Resources

**Varies by site.** Generally:
- Site practicum manual
- Relevant assessment instruments
- DSM-5-TR
- HKPS Code of Professional Conduct

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Practicum | Supervised clinical placement |
| Direct service | Face-to-face client contact |
| Support activity | Indirect client work (notes, prep) |
| Supervisor | Licensed psychologist overseeing work |
| Mental status exam | Structured clinical assessment |
| Risk assessment | Evaluation of self-harm/harm-to-others risk |
| Case formulation | Integrative understanding of client |
| Live observation | Real-time observation by supervisor |
| Reflective journal | Written reflection on practice |
| Competency | Specific knowledge or skill |

## Self-Check Questions

1. What were the main learning objectives of Practicum I? Were they met?
2. Describe a case you worked on. What did you learn?
3. How did supervision contribute to your learning?
4. What ethical issues arose? How did you handle them?
5. What are your goals for Practicum II?

## Capstone Connection

Practicum I provides initial clinical experience that informs the dissertation topic selection and PSU In-House Practicum.
