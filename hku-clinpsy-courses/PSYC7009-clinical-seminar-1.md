# PSYC7009 — Clinical Seminar I

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7009
- **Title:** Clinical seminar I
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Case-based seminar integrating assessment, formulation, and treatment planning. Students present and discuss cases from their practicum placements. Faculty and peer feedback develop clinical reasoning and case presentation skills.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7029
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (case presentations, participation)
- **Notes:** Year 1 case conference series

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Present cases clearly and comprehensively
2. Apply biopsychosocial formulation
3. Develop evidence-based treatment plans
4. Integrate theory, research, and practice
5. Provide constructive peer feedback
6. Discuss ethical and professional issues in cases
7. Apply DSM-5 diagnostic reasoning

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Case Presentation Skills (3 hours)
- Structure of clinical case presentation
- Identifying and presenting key information
- Concise but comprehensive presentations

### Topic 2: Biopsychosocial Formulation (5 hours)
- Predisposing, precipitating, perpetuating, protective
- Cognitive case formulation (Beck)
- Personality formulation
- Cultural formulation

### Topic 3: Diagnostic Reasoning (4 hours)
- Differential diagnosis
- Use of structured interviews
- Considering comorbidities
- Cultural factors

### Topic 4: Treatment Planning (5 hours)
- SMART goals
- Evidence-based interventions
- Sequencing of interventions
- Outcome monitoring

### Topic 5: Special Populations (8 hours)
- Children and adolescents
- Older adults
- Culturally diverse clients
- Severe mental illness

### Topic 6: Common Presenting Problems (8 hours)
- Depression
- Anxiety
- OCD
- Trauma
- Substance use

### Topic 7: Ethical and Professional Issues (3 hours)
- Boundary issues
- Confidentiality
- Risk management
- Cultural considerations

### Topic 8: Integration and Reflection (3 hours)
- Linking theory, research, practice
- Self-reflection
- Professional development

### Topic 9: Peer Feedback Skills (3 hours)
- Constructive feedback
- Receiving feedback
- Group facilitation

### Topic 10: Final Presentation (3 hours)
- Year 1 portfolio case
- Comprehensive presentation
- Self-evaluation

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Case presentation | Structured presentation of case |
| Biopsychosocial | Biological + psychological + social |
| Predisposing | Pre-existing risk factors |
| Precipitating | Triggering factors |
| Perpetuating | Maintaining factors |
| Protective | Resilience factors |
| SMART goals | Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound |
| Evidence-based | Supported by research |
| Formulation | Integrative understanding |
| DSM-5-TR | Diagnostic manual |
| Differential diagnosis | Distinguishing conditions |
| Cultural formulation | Framework for cultural context |

2. Develop SMART goals for a treatment plan.
3. Apply evidence-based treatment selection.
