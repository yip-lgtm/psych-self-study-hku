# PSYC8011 — Practicum III

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8011
- **Title:** Practicum III
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Third supervised clinical practicum. Increased complexity and autonomy. May include specialty populations (e.g., severe mental illness, neuropsychology, child and adolescent). Continued development of professional competencies.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7030
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Year 2 practicum; specialty focus

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Manage a complex caseload with increasing autonomy
2. Apply specialized clinical skills
3. Conduct comprehensive assessments for specialty populations
4. Deliver advanced interventions
5. Demonstrate professional competencies expected at mid-program
6. Engage in supervision of various types
7. Manage professional challenges

## Topic Outline
