# PSYC8014 — Psychological Services Unit (PSU) In-House Practicum

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8014
- **Title:** Psychological Services Unit In-House Practicum
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2 (typically final semester, capstone)
- **HKU Description:** The PSU is the department's training clinic where students provide psychological services under close supervision to individuals, couples, families, and groups from the community. This final practicum integrates all prior training in a supportive environment with high supervision intensity. Students manage a caseload, conduct assessments, and provide evidence-based interventions. This is the culminating clinical experience.
- **Prerequisites:** All Year 1 courses + PSYC8011, PSYC8012, PSYC8013
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (clinical work, supervisor evaluation, case presentations, reflective portfolio)
- **Notes:** Capstone clinical experience; department training clinic

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Manage a diverse clinical caseload independently
2. Conduct comprehensive assessments
3. Deliver evidence-based individual and group interventions
4. Integrate systemic perspectives
5. Apply ethical principles in complex situations
6. Demonstrate entry-level professional competency
7. Show readiness for entry-level practice as a clinical psychologist

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: PSU Orientation (4 hours)
- Clinic policies and procedures
- Intake and scheduling
- Record-keeping
- Emergency protocols

### Topic 2: Clinical Caseload Management (50-80 hours)
- Intake assessments
- Individual therapy cases
- Case formulation and treatment planning
- Progress notes
- Outcome monitoring

### Topic 3: Assessment (20-40 hours)
- Comprehensive assessments
- Personality testing
- Cognitive testing
- Report writing
- Feedback sessions

### Topic 4: Group Therapy (10-20 hours)
- Co-facilitate groups
- Skills groups
- Support groups

### Topic 5: Family/Couples Work (5-15 hours, as available)
- Systemic formulation
- Family sessions
- Couples work

### Topic 6: Individual Supervision (weekly, 1-2 hours)
- Primary supervisor
- Case review
- Video/audio review
- Direct observation

### Topic 7: Group Supervision (weekly, 1-2 hours)
- Peer case consultation
- Case conference presentations
- Didactic seminars

### Topic 8: Case Conference Presentations (2-3 presentations)
- Comprehensive case presentation
- Formulation
- Treatment planning
- Peer and faculty feedback

### Topic 9: Professional Development (5-10 hours)
- Reading and self-study
- Presentation to peers
- Preparation for entry-level practice

### Topic 10: Portfolio and Self-Evaluation (ongoing)
- Reflective portfolio
- Competency self-assessment
- Goal setting
- Post-PSU plan

### Topic 11: Transition to Practice (5 hours)
- Job search strategies
- Licensure preparation (HKPS DCP)
- Continuing professional development
- Professional networks
