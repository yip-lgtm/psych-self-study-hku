# PSYC8012 — Practicum IV

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8012
- **Title:** Practicum IV
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Fourth supervised clinical practicum. Continues development in specialty area with further autonomy. May include intensive intervention work (e.g., full CBT protocols, manualized treatments).
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC8011
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Year 2 practicum

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Deliver full evidence-based treatment protocols
2. Manage treatment-resistant cases
3. Apply advanced specialty skills
4. Demonstrate near-entry-level competency
5. Supervise and mentor junior trainees
6. Engage in advanced supervision
7. Plan for entry-level practice

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (if new site)
- Site policies
- Specialty focus

### Topic 2: Full Protocol Implementation (40–60 hours)
- CBT for depression (16-20 sessions)
- CBT for anxiety (12-16 sessions)
- Trauma-focused CBT
- Other manualized treatments

### Topic 3: Advanced Cases (40–60 hours)
- Treatment-resistant depression
- Comorbid presentations
- Complex trauma
- Personality disorder cases

### Topic 4: Group Interventions (10–20 hours)
- Group CBT
- Skills groups (DBT)
- Support groups

### Topic 5: Consultation and Liaison (10–15 hours)
- Multidisciplinary teams
- Consultation to other professionals
- Crisis consultation

### Topic 6: Supervision (weekly)
- Decreasing supervision
- Increased autonomy
- Specialty supervision

### Topic 7: Mentoring Junior Trainees (5–10 hours)
- Peer mentoring
- Co-leading groups
- Sharing clinical knowledge

### Topic 8: Outcome and Quality (5 hours)
- Outcome monitoring
- Quality assurance
- Program development input

### Topic 9: Professional Identity (5 hours)
- Identity as clinical psychologist
- Career planning
- Networking

### Topic 10: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 11: Case Conference Presentation (8 hours)

### Topic 12: Evaluation (3 hours)
