# PSYC7006 — Psychopathology II

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC7006
- **Title:** Psychopathology II
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Continuation of Psychopathology I. Covers severe mental illness, personality disorders, eating disorders, child/adolescent disorders, and disorders across the lifespan. Advanced differential diagnosis and complex case formulation.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7005
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Advanced psychopathology; severe mental illness focus

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Diagnose severe mental illness (SMI)
2. Apply personality disorder frameworks
3. Discuss eating disorders
4. Describe child/adolescent disorders
5. Apply lifespan developmental psychopathology
6. Conduct complex case formulation
7. Integrate cultural and family factors

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Schizophrenia Spectrum (8 hours)
- Schizophrenia
- Schizoaffective disorder
- Schizophreniform
- Brief psychotic disorder
- Delusional disorder
- Attenuated psychosis syndrome (proposed)

### Topic 2: Bipolar and Related (3 hours, advanced)
- Mixed features specifier
- Rapid cycling
- Treatment-resistant cases

### Topic 3: Personality Disorders — DSM-5 Section III (6 hours)
- Alternative Model for PDs (Criterion A: personality functioning)
- Five personality disorder trait domains
- Personality Disorder-Trait Specified
- Comparison with DSM-5 Section II categorical

### Topic 4: Personality Disorders — Specific Types (8 hours)
- Borderline
- Narcissistic
- Antisocial
- Avoidant
- Dependent
- Obsessive-compulsive
- Schizoid/schizotypal

### Topic 5: Eating Disorders (4 hours)
- Anorexia nervosa
- Bulimia nervosa
- Binge eating disorder
- ARFID (Avoidant/Restrictive Food Intake)
- Body dysmorphic considerations

### Topic 6: Dissociative Disorders (3 hours)
- Dissociative amnesia
- Depersonalization/derealization
- DID (Dissociative Identity Disorder)

### Topic 7: Child and Adolescent Disorders I — Neurodevelopmental (6 hours)
- ADHD
- Autism Spectrum Disorder
- Intellectual disability
- Specific learning disorders
- Motor disorders
- Tic disorders (Tourette's)

### Topic 8: Child and Adolescent Disorders II — Other (5 hours)
- Conduct disorder
- Oppositional defiant
- Anxiety in children
- Childhood depression
- Childhood PTSD

### Topic 9: Neurocognitive Disorders (3 hours)
- Delirium
- Major and mild NCD
- Alzheimer's
- Vascular dementia
- Frontotemporal
- Lewy body

### Topic 10: Complex Case Formulation (4 hours)
- Multi-axis integration
- Family system factors
- Cultural factors
- Treatment recommendations
