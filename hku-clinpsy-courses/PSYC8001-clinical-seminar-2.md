# PSYC8001 — Clinical Seminar II

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8001
- **Title:** Clinical seminar II
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Advanced case-based seminar integrating complex cases from Year 2 practicum. Focus on integration of multiple theoretical orientations, evidence-based practice, professional issues, and preparation for entry-level practice.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7009
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Year 2 case conference series

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Present complex cases with sophisticated formulation
2. Apply integrative case conceptualization
3. Critically evaluate evidence base
4. Address professional and ethical issues
5. Demonstrate clinical leadership
6. Prepare for entry-level practice
7. Integrate research and practice

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Advanced Case Presentation (3 hours)
- Complex multi-problem cases
- Integration across orientations
- Cultural and systemic factors

### Topic 2: Integrative Case Conceptualization (6 hours)
- Multi-orientation formulation
- Selecting interventions from multiple models
- Sequencing and pacing

### Topic 3: Evidence-Based Practice (6 hours)
- EBP tripartite model (best evidence + clinical expertise + patient values)
- Critically appraising literature
- Applying to individual cases
- Stepped care

### Topic 4: Complex Cases — Severe Mental Illness (5 hours)
- Schizophrenia
- Bipolar disorder
- Treatment-resistant depression
- Risk management

### Topic 5: Complex Cases — Personality Disorders (5 hours)
- BPD
- Narcissistic PD
- Treatment planning
- Therapy relationship challenges

### Topic 6: Complex Cases — Trauma (4 hours)
- Complex PTSD
- Dissociative disorders
- Phase-oriented treatment

### Topic 7: Cultural and Diversity Issues (3 hours)
- HK Chinese context
- Cross-cultural work
- Linguistic considerations
- Refugee and migrant populations

### Topic 8: Professional and Ethical Issues (4 hours)
- Complex ethical dilemmas
- Boundary issues
- Confidentiality limits
- Risk management
- Professional communication

### Topic 9: Clinical Leadership (3 hours)
- Leading teams
- Consultation skills
- Program development

### Topic 10: Preparation for Entry-Level Practice (4 hours)
- Licensure requirements (HKPS DCP)
- Job search
- Career planning
- Continuing professional development

### Topic 11: Final Portfolio Presentation (5 hours)
- Comprehensive portfolio case
- Self-evaluation
- Program reflection

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Integrative | Combining multiple approaches |
| EBP | Evidence-based practice |
| Stepped care | Treatment intensity matched to need |
| Phase-oriented | Sequenced treatment phases |
| Complex PTSD | Result of prolonged trauma |
| Dissociation | Disruption of consciousness |
| Risk management | Strategies to mitigate risk |
| Boundary | Professional limit |
| Consultation | Professional advice |
| Leadership | Influencing others toward goals |
| Licensure | Regulatory authorization |
