# PSYC8006 — Dissertation

## Course Info

- **Code:** PSYC8006
- **Title:** Dissertation
- **Program:** MSocSc in Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** 2 (full year or summer)
- **HKU Description:** Independent empirical research project under faculty supervision. The dissertation should make an original contribution to knowledge in clinical psychology and demonstrate competence in research design, data collection, analysis, and scientific writing.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7019, PSYC7020
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework (thesis submission + oral defense/viva)
- **Notes:** The capstone research project

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Conduct independent empirical research
2. Develop a research question and proposal
3. Apply appropriate research design and methods
4. Collect and analyze data
5. Write a scientific thesis
6. Defend your research in a viva
7. Contribute to knowledge in clinical psychology

## Topic Outline

### Phase 1: Proposal Development (Month 1-3)
- Topic selection
- Literature review
- Research question and hypotheses
- Method design
- Ethics application (IRB)
- Proposal defense

### Phase 2: Data Collection (Month 3-9)
- Ethics approval
- Recruitment
- Data collection
- Data management
- Regular supervision meetings

### Phase 3: Analysis and Writing (Month 9-15)
- Data analysis
- Results writing
- Discussion
- Literature integration
- APA 7th formatting
- Revision

### Phase 4: Submission and Defense (Month 15-18)
- Final thesis submission
- Viva voce examination
- Revision (if required)
- Deposit
