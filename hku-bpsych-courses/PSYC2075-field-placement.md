# PSYC2075 — Field Placement

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC2075
- **Title:** Field placement
- **Credits:** 12
- **HKU Catalog Description:** (For psychology as a first major) Students will begin their placement after completing the intensive training workshops. They will be placed in NGOs, government agencies, or other mental health/psychology-focused public/private organisations during term time or summer. They will be assessed by both an academic tutor and a workplace supervisor.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC1001 and PSYC1004
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** 12 credits (double); placement in real org; both academic and workplace assessment

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply psychological knowledge in real organizational settings
2. Develop professional skills in psychology-related work
3. Demonstrate ethical and culturally sensitive practice
4. Reflect on professional development
5. Build professional networks
6. Make informed career decisions
7. Communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders

## Topic Outline

**The structure varies by placement. The following is a representative structure.**

### Topic 1: Pre-Placement Preparation (10-15 hours)
- Application and selection
- Background checks (if applicable)
- Orientation to placement organization
- Setting learning objectives

### Topic 2: Initial Training Workshops (10-20 hours)
- Professional ethics
- Confidentiality
- Cultural competence
- Communication skills
- Crisis management

### Topic 3: Placement Activity — Direct Service (60-100 hours)
- Working with clients/participants
- Assessment or screening
- Intervention delivery
- Documentation

### Topic 4: Placement Activity — Indirect Service (30-50 hours)
- Program development
- Research support
- Consultation
- Administrative tasks

### Topic 5: Supervision (weekly, 1-2 hours each)
- Individual supervision
- Group supervision
- Feedback and reflection
- Skill development

### Topic 6: Mid-Placement Review (5-10 hours)
- Progress assessment
- Adjustment of goals
- Addressing challenges
- Both academic and workplace feedback

### Topic 7: Final Placement Report (20-30 hours)
- Comprehensive report on activities
- Reflection on learning objectives
- Connection to psychological theory and research
- Future professional development plan

### Topic 8: Final Presentation (5-10 hours)
- Presentation to academic and/or workplace audience
- Demonstration of learning
- Peer learning
