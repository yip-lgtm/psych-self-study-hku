# PSYC4011 — Special Topics in Psychology V (Mental Health and Clinical Science)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC4011
- **Title:** Special topics in psychology V (mental health and clinical science)
- **Credits:** 6
- **HKU Catalog Description:** This advanced seminar course focuses on a specific topic at the cutting edge of psychology research. Topic varies by offering. Possible topics include clinical science, RDoC framework, mechanisms of psychotherapy, transdiagnostic approaches, suicide research, digital mental health, and other emerging areas. May involve substantial reading, original research, and presentation. Restricted to final-year BPsych students.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC1004, PSYC2062, PSYC2010
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** 4000-level honours seminar; clinical focus

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Engage deeply with clinical science at the research frontier
2. Discuss the RDoC framework
3. Apply transdiagnostic approaches
4. Discuss mechanisms of psychotherapy
5. Analyze suicide research
6. Discuss digital mental health
7. Critically evaluate clinical science

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Clinical science | Research-based clinical psychology |
| RDoC | Research Domain Criteria |
| Transdiagnostic | Across diagnoses |
| Network theory | Disorders as networks of symptoms |
| Common factors | Elements shared by therapies |
| Dodo bird verdict | All therapies equally effective |
| Suicide | Self-inflicted death |
| Idiom of distress | Culture-specific expression |
| Cultural formulation | DSM-5 cultural framework |
| Stigma | Negative attitudes/discrimination |
| Prevention science | Study of prevention |
| Digital mental health | Tech-based MH interventions |

## Self-Check Questions

2. Discuss the case for transdiagnostic approaches.
3. What is the evidence for the Dodo bird verdict?
4. Evaluate digital mental health interventions.
5. Discuss suicide prevention in HK Chinese context.
