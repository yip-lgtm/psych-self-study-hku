# PSYC4008 — Special Topics in Psychology II (Personality and Health)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC4008
- **Title:** Special topics in psychology II (personality and health)
- **Credits:** 6
- **HKU Catalog Description:** This advanced seminar course focuses on a specific topic at the cutting edge of psychology research. Topic varies by offering. Possible topics include personality and health, self-regulation, health behavior change, psychoneuroimmunology, behavioral medicine, and other emerging areas. May involve substantial reading, original research, and presentation. Restricted to final-year BPsych students.
- **Prerequisites:** Variable, typically Year 3 standing and 2000-level foundation
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** 4000-level honours seminar; topic varies

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Engage deeply with a specialized topic at the research frontier
2. Read and synthesize primary literature in the area
3. Conduct original research or analysis
4. Present research findings
5. Critically evaluate theoretical and methodological issues
6. Connect specialized topic to broader psychology
