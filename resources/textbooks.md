# Resources — Textbooks (Free / Low-Cost)

## Tier 1 — Primary Reading (free, start here)

### OpenStax Psychology 2e
- **URL:** https://openstax.org/details/books/psychology-2e
- **Use:** Intro to Psychology (Month 1)
- **Coverage:** ~95% of HKU PSYC1001 content
- **Format:** Free PDF, online, or print (low cost)

### Noba Project
- **URL:** https://noba.to
- **Use:** All modules except advanced stats
- **Coverage:** Bio, Cog, Dev, Social, Personality, Abnormal, Health, Learning
- **Format:** Modular, expert-authored, free

### "Learning Statistics with JASP"
- **Author:** Navarro & Foxcroft
- **Use:** Stats foundation (Month 1–2)
- **Format:** Free PDF

---

## Tier 2 — Deepening (free or library loan)

### Biopsychology
- **Pinel, "Biopsychology"** (selected chapters on neuroanatomy, neurotransmitters, behavioral genetics)
- **Alternative:** "Neuroscience" by Purves et al. (free via some university library access)

### Cognitive Psychology
- **Goldstein, "Cognitive Psychology"** (ch 1–6 covers perception, attention, memory)
- **Alternative:** MIT OCW 9.70 "Vision & Thought"

### Personality
- **Noba Personality module** (primary)
- **Reference:** "Personality Psychology: Domains of Knowledge" by Larsen, Buss, et al. (selected chapters)

### Social Psychology
- **Noba Social module** (primary)
- **Reference:** "The Story of Social Psychology" by Baumeister & Bushman (free PDF available)

### Developmental
- **Noba Developmental module** (primary)
- **Reference:** "Lifespan Development" by Lally & Valentine-French (free PDF)

### Psychopathology / Clinical
- **Noba Psychopathology module**
- **Reference:** DSM-5-TR (library reference only — not a textbook)

---

## Tier 3 — Subfield Deepening (Month 4 onward)

### Judgment & Decision Making
- Kahneman, "Thinking, Fast and Slow" (popular)
- **Academic:** Kahneman & Tversky collected papers; "Judgment and Decision Making" by Newell, Lagnado, Shanks
- **Key journals:** *Judgment and Decision Making*, *Cognitive Science*

### Health Psychology
- Taylor, "Health Psychology" (selected chapters)
- **Key journals:** *Health Psychology*, *Psychology & Health*, *Annals of Behavioral Medicine*

### Quantitative / Psychometrics
- Furr, "Psychometrics: An Introduction" (3rd ed.)
- **Key reference:** "Measurement Theory and Applications" by Furr
- **Key journals:** *Psychometrika*, *Multivariate Behavioral Research*, *Behavior Research Methods*

### Educational Psychology
- Ormrod, "Educational Psychology: Developing Learners" (selected)
- **Key journals:** *Educational Psychology Review*, *Learning and Instruction*

### Cognitive Neuroscience / Human-AI Interaction
- Gazzaniga, "Cognitive Neuroscience" (selected)
- **Key journals:** *Cognitive Neuropsychology*, *Cognition*, *Trends in Cognitive Sciences*

## Paper Search & Access (free)

| Tool | Use |
|---|---|
| **Google Scholar** | General search, citation tracking |
| **Semantic Scholar** | AI-powered search, paper summaries |
| **PsyArXiv** | Preprints (latest research, free) |
| **OSF** | Open Science Framework — datasets + preregistrations |
| **Unpaywall** | Browser extension — find legal free PDFs |
| **PubMed** | Neuroscience / health psychology crossover |
| **Z-Library** | Use cautiously; prefer library loan |
