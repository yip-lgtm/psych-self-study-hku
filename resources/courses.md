# Resources — Free Online Courses

## Month 1 (start here)

### Coursera — Yale "Introduction to Psychology"
- **URL:** https://www.coursera.org/learn/introduction-to-psychology
- **Format:** Audit for free (no certificate)
- **Time:** ~16 hours total
- **Why:** Structured, covers all major subfields, complements OpenStax

### Khan Academy — Statistics
- **URL:** https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability
- **Format:** Free, self-paced
- **Use:** Refresh basic stats (your edge: should fly through this)
