## How to Reach Out (Month 5 task)

### Email Template (Cold Outreach to Faculty)

```
Subject: Prospective MSocSc(Psy) Applicant — Research Alignment Question

Dear Professor [Name],

I'm a structural engineer planning to apply to the HKU MSocSc(Psy) programme
for 2027 intake. I'm currently completing a 6-month self-study in
psychology (covering BPsych core + research methods) and designing a small
empirical capstone project.

Your work on [specific paper or research area] strongly aligns with my
interests in [your subfield]. I'd be grateful for 15 minutes of your time
to ask:

1. Whether my proposed research direction [1-sentence summary] would be
   feasible in your lab context.
2. What skills/background you'd most value in a research assistant.
3. Any suggestions for the application itself.

I understand you're busy; I'm happy to work around your schedule. A brief
Zoom or in-person meeting would be ideal.

Thank you for considering,
Saba
[LinkedIn] [GitHub for capstone]
```

### Send Timing
- **Best:** Sept–Oct 2026 (4–5 months before deadline)
- **Avoid:** Dec 2026 (faculty are slammed with semester-end)
- **Follow up:** 1 nudge after 2 weeks if no response; then move on
