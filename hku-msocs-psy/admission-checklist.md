## Personal Statement — Structure (1000 words)

### Paragraph 1 — Hook (150 words)
- Your "why psychology" moment
- Be specific: a conversation, a book, a project that shifted you
- Engineer → psychologist is unusual; lean into it

### Paragraph 2 — What You Bring (200 words)
- BEng quantitative training
- **Real-world decision experience:** slope stability, Minor Works, tender preparation
- Multi-disciplinary self-learning (MMA, AI agents, trading, etc.)
- Specific psychology interests: name subfields, key papers, ideas

### Paragraph 3 — What You've Done (250 words)
- The 6-month self-study
- The capstone project
- Stats + methods competency (JASP + R + **Python** — your Data Science cert is an edge)
- Specific skills (survey design, factor analysis, regression, behavioral coding)

### Paragraph 4 — Why MSocSc(Psy) (200 words)
- What the program provides that self-study cannot
- Cohort, supervision, lab access, formal assessment
- Be honest about your gaps; show self-awareness

### Paragraph 5 — Why HKU (150 words)
- Named faculty member(s) whose research aligns with yours
- Specific lab / centre / project you want to join
- HK location, your network, your commitment to staying in HK

### Paragraph 6 — Future (50 words)
- After MSocSc: MPhil/PhD in [subfield] / DPsych Clinical
- Career vision: where you want to be in 7–10 years

## Research Proposal — Structure (1 page)

### Title
- Specific, descriptive, contains key variables

### Background (150 words)
- Why this question matters
- Gap in literature
- 2–3 key citations

### Research Question (50 words)
- Clear, focused, testable

### Method (200 words)
- Participants (N, recruitment)
- Materials (measures, instruments)
- Procedure
- Planned analysis (which inferential tests + effect sizes)

### Significance (100 words)
- Theoretical contribution
- Practical implications
- Connection to your longer-term research agenda

### References
- 8–15 references, APA 7th

## Reference Letter Strategy

### Referee 1 — Academic
- Ideally someone who taught you in BEng (can comment on intellectual ability)
- Or: a Coursera instructor (rarely possible — use only if you have a real connection)
- **Best:** an HKU faculty member you've emailed (after 1 meeting)

### Referee 2 — Professional
- Your manager / senior engineer
- Can speak to: project management, problem-solving, working under pressure
- **Specifically ask them to mention:** decision-making under uncertainty (slope/tender/Minor Works), self-learning initiative
- Brief them on the psychology angle; ask them to highlight transferable skills

### Referee Prep Email Template
```
Hi [Name],

I'm applying to the HKU MSocSc(Psy) conversion programme (1-year full-time, starting Sep 2027).

Would you be willing to write a reference letter? The deadline is [date].

The programme is looking for candidates who can:
- Bridge quantitative skills to psychology
- Demonstrate self-directed research capability
- Contribute to a research cohort

If you're willing, I'll send you my Personal Statement + CV + capstone abstract so the letter can be specific.

Thanks for considering!
Saba
```

## Common Pitfalls

- ❌ Generic PS ("I've always been interested in people...") — be SPECIFIC
- ❌ Over-emphasizing engineering; under-explaining psychology
- ❌ No faculty mention (signals you haven't researched the program)
- ❌ Typos / grammar issues (use a real human proofreader)
- ❌ Sending forms late (HKU is strict on deadlines)
- ❌ Forgetting to update your referees once you submit (good etiquette)
- ❌ Forgetting to mention your Python / Data Science cert (it's a differentiator)

---

## If You Get Rejected

1. **Ask for feedback** (rarely given but worth asking)
2. **Reapply next year** with stronger capstone + 1 published blog post or conference poster
3. **Pivot to BPsych** (4-year route) if the conversion path remains blocked — see `bpsych-as-alternative.md`
4. **Apply to other programs** in parallel (CUHK, HKBU, PolyU all have conversion options)
