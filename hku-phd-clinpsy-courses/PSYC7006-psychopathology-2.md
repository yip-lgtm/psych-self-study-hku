# PSYC7006 — Psychopathology II (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7006
- **Title:** Psychopathology II
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level continuation of Psychopathology I. Severe mental illness, personality disorders, eating disorders, child/adolescent disorders. Advanced differential diagnosis, complex case formulation, research integration.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7005
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level advanced psychopathology

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Diagnose SMI with sophistication
2. Apply personality disorder frameworks with research literacy
3. Discuss eating disorders at doctoral level
4. Describe child/adolescent disorders with research integration
5. Apply lifespan developmental psychopathology
6. Conduct doctoral-level complex case formulation
7. Integrate cultural and family factors
8. Engage with classification debates

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Schizophrenia Spectrum (8 hours)
- Doctoral-level schizophrenia
- Schizoaffective
- Schizophreniform, brief psychotic, delusional
- Attenuated psychosis syndrome
- Research on psychosis

### Topic 2: Bipolar and Related Advanced (3 hours)
- Mixed features specifier
- Rapid cycling
- Treatment-resistant
- Research

### Topic 3: Personality Disorders — DSM-5 Section III (6 hours)
- Doctoral-level Alternative Model
- Five personality disorder trait domains
- PD-TS
- Section II vs. III comparison
- Research on PDs

### Topic 4: Personality Disorders — Specific Types (8 hours)
- Doctoral-level borderline
- Narcissistic, antisocial
- Avoidant, dependent
- OCPD
- Schizoid/schizotypal
- Research on each

### Topic 5: Eating Disorders (4 hours)
- Doctoral-level anorexia, bulimia
- BED
- ARFID
- Research on EDs

### Topic 6: Dissociative Disorders (3 hours)
- Dissociative amnesia
- Depersonalization/derealization
- DID
- Research on dissociation

### Topic 7: Child and Adolescent Disorders I — Neurodevelopmental (6 hours)
- Doctoral-level ADHD
- ASD
- Intellectual disability
- Specific learning disorders
- Motor and tic disorders
- Research

### Topic 8: Child and Adolescent Disorders II — Other (5 hours)
- Conduct, ODD
- Childhood anxiety and depression
- Childhood PTSD
- Research

### Topic 9: Neurocognitive Disorders (3 hours)
- Doctoral-level delirium
- Major and mild NCD
- Alzheimer's
- Vascular, FTD, Lewy body
- Research

### Topic 10: Complex Case Formulation (4 hours)
- Doctoral-level multi-axis integration
- Family system factors
- Cultural factors
- Research-informed recommendations

### Topic 11: Classification Debates (3 hours)
- DSM vs. RDoC vs. network
- Transdiagnostic approaches
- HiTOP
- Doctoral-level perspective
