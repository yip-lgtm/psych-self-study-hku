# PSYC8011 — Practicum III (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8011
- **Title:** Practicum III
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Third supervised clinical practicum. Doctoral-level complexity, autonomy, and specialty focus. Advanced clinical skills and research-practice integration.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7030
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level specialty focus

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Manage a complex doctoral-level caseload
2. Apply doctoral-level specialty clinical skills
3. Conduct comprehensive assessments for specialty populations
4. Deliver advanced interventions
5. Demonstrate doctoral-level professional competencies
6. Engage in doctoral-level supervision
7. Manage professional challenges
8. Integrate research and practice
