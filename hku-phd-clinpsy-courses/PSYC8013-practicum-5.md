# PSYC8013 — Practicum V (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8013
- **Title:** Practicum V
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Final pre-PSU supervised clinical practicum. Entry-level doctoral competency expected. Final integration of clinical skills in preparation for PSU capstone.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC8012
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Final practicum before PSU

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Demonstrate entry-level doctoral clinical competencies
2. Manage a full doctoral-level caseload independently
3. Apply integrated assessment and treatment
4. Engage in peer supervision at doctoral level
5. Plan for PSU
6. Demonstrate doctoral-level professional autonomy
7. Develop entry-level doctoral career plan

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (if new site)

### Topic 2: Full Caseload Management (60-90 hours)
- Doctoral-level independent case management
- Comprehensive services
- Coordination of care

### Topic 3: Doctoral-Level Advanced Integration (40-50 hours)
- Integrating multiple modalities
- Complex decision-making
- Risk management
- Research integration

- Co-leading supervision group
- Mentoring junior trainees
- Sharing doctoral-level clinical insights

### Topic 5: Specialty Concentration (40-60 hours)
- Specialty area deepening
- Doctoral-level evidence-based practice

### Topic 6: Consultation and Liaison (10-15 hours)

### Topic 7: Doctoral-Level Professional Practice (10 hours)
- Documentation quality
- Communication
- Time management
- Self-care

### Topic 8: Outcome and Program Development (5-8 hours)

### Topic 9: Supervision (weekly)
- Decreasing intensity
- Doctoral-level transition to peer consultation

### Topic 10: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 11: Final Case Conference (8-10 hours)
- Doctoral-level comprehensive case
- Integration of all training

### Topic 12: Pre-PSU Evaluation (3 hours)
- Doctoral-level comprehensive competency
- Goal setting for PSU
