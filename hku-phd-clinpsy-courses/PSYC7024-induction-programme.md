# PSYC7024 — Induction Programme (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7024
- **Title:** Induction programme
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1 (entry)
- **HKU Description:** Orientation to the PhD Clinical Psychology program, faculty research, clinical training sites, scientific-practitioner model, and doctoral-level expectations. Sets the foundation for the four-year training program.
- **Prerequisites:** Program admission
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** PhD-level orientation; researcher-practitioner model

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Describe the scientist-practitioner model at doctoral level
2. Articulate your research interests and potential dissertation topics
3. Navigate program resources (faculty, supervisors, sites)
4. Discuss ethical and professional frameworks at doctoral level
5. Apply advanced self-care and resilience strategies
6. Demonstrate professional identity as a doctoral clinical psychologist
7. Plan the 4-year training trajectory

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Program Overview (4 hours)
- Scientist-practitioner model (Boulder)
- 4-year structure and milestones
- Coursework, practicum, dissertation
- Comprehensive exams

### Topic 2: Profession of Clinical Psychology at Doctoral Level (5 hours)
- History of doctoral training
- Scientist-practitioner tradition
- Clinical science model (Boulder vs. Vail)
- Career trajectories (research, clinical, academic, policy)

### Topic 3: Ethical Framework at Doctoral Level (5 hours)
- HKPS Code of Professional Conduct
- APA Ethics Code
- Mental Health Ordinance (HK Cap 136)
- Advanced ethical reasoning
- Ethical dimensions of research

### Topic 4: Research Foundations (5 hours)
- Faculty research areas
- Lab rotations
- Identifying dissertation topic
- Building research portfolio

### Topic 5: Self-Care and Resilience (4 hours)
- Vicarious trauma in clinical work
- Researcher burnout
- Compassion fatigue
- Mindfulness for clinicians
- Supervision as self-care

### Topic 6: Diversity and Cultural Humility (4 hours)
- HK multicultural context
- Cross-cultural research
- Cultural competence in research and practice
- Indigenous psychologies

### Topic 7: Advanced Clinical Skills Foundation (4 hours)
- Observation and listening (advanced)
- Reflective practice
- Case formulation at doctoral level
- Professional writing for research and clinical

### Topic 8: Comprehensive Exam Overview (3 hours)
- Purpose and structure
- Preparation strategies
- Timeline

### Topic 9: Dissertation Planning (4 hours)
- Topic selection
- Reading groups
- Building research ideas
- Time management for 4 years

### Topic 10: Career Planning (2 hours)
- Academic career path
- Clinical research career
- Private practice
- Industry and policy
- Networking

## Key Concepts Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Scientist-practitioner | Integrating research and practice |
| Boulder model | Doctoral training model |
| Vail model | Practitioner-scholar model |
| Clinical science | Research-based clinical psychology |
| Lab rotation | Initial research exposure |
| Dissertation | Original research project |
| Comprehensive exam | Program milestone |
| Vicarious trauma | Indirect trauma from clients |
| Cultural humility | Ongoing cultural self-evaluation |
| Reflective practice | Critical self-examination |
| Research portfolio | Cumulative research record |
