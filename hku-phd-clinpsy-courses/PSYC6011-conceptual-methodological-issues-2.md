# PSYC6011 — Conceptual and Methodological Issues in Psychological Research II

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC6011
- **Title:** Conceptual and methodological issues in psychological research II
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Continuation of PSYC6010. Advanced methodological issues: causal inference, complexity theory, computational modeling, neuroscience-method integration, and contemporary methodological debates in clinical psychology research.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC6010
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Builds on PSYC6010; advanced methods focus

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply causal inference frameworks
2. Discuss complexity theory
3. Apply computational modeling
4. Integrate neuroscience and behavioral methods
5. Critically evaluate contemporary methods
6. Design rigorous doctoral-level research

## Topic Outline

### Topic 1: Review of PSYC6010 (3 hours)
- Key philosophical positions
- Implications for research

### Topic 2: Causal Inference (6 hours)
- Counterfactual framework
- Directed acyclic graphs (DAGs)
- Instrumental variables
- Natural experiments
- Mediation and moderation

### Topic 3: Complexity Theory (5 hours)
- Complex systems
- Emergence
- Non-linear dynamics
- Application to psychopathology

### Topic 4: Computational Modeling (5 hours)
- Reinforcement learning
- Drift diffusion
- Bayesian models
- Model-based vs. model-free
- Model comparison

### Topic 5: Network Theory of Psychopathology (4 hours)
- Disorders as networks
- Estimation
- Stability
- Clinical implications

### Topic 6: Neuroscience-Method Integration (5 hours)
- Neuroimaging in clinical research
- Combining behavioral and neural measures
- ML on neuroimaging data
- Mobile neuroimaging

### Topic 7: Precision Medicine (4 hours)
- RDoC framework
- Biomarkers
- Personalized treatment
- Implementation challenges

### Topic 8: Ecological Momentary Assessment (3 hours)
- EMA methods
- Experience sampling
- Smartphone-based assessment
- Data analysis

### Topic 9: Digital Phenotyping (3 hours)
- Passive sensing
- Social media
- Smartphone data
- Ethical issues

### Topic 10: Implementation Science (3 hours)
- Bridging research and practice
- Implementation frameworks
- Hybrid designs

### Topic 11: Methodological Debates (4 hours)
- Null hypothesis significance testing controversies
- Bayesian alternatives
- Pre-registration debate
- Open science
- Replication in clinical psychology

### Topic 12: Doctoral Research Design Workshop (3 hours)
- Refining your dissertation
- Integrating methods
- Defending choices

## Self-Check Questions

1. Apply DAGs to a clinical research question.
2. Discuss complexity theory in psychopathology.
3. Compare network theory and traditional views of disorder.
4. What is precision medicine in clinical psychology?
5. Critically evaluate a recent methodological debate.
