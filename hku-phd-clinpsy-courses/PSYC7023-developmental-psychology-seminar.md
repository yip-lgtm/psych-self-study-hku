# PSYC7023 — Postgraduate Seminar in Developmental Psychology

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7023
- **Title:** Postgraduate seminar in developmental psychology
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology; elective for MSocSc Clinical Psychology
- **Year:** Variable
- **HKU Description:** Advanced graduate seminar in developmental psychology. Current research and theory in cognitive, social, emotional, and neural development across the lifespan. Emphasis on critical evaluation of methods and theory, with connections to clinical applications.
- **Prerequisites:** Postgrad standing; foundation in developmental psychology
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** PhD elective; advanced research focus

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Critically evaluate contemporary developmental psychology research
2. Discuss current theoretical debates
3. Apply advanced methods in developmental research
4. Connect developmental psychology to clinical applications
5. Design rigorous developmental research
6. Communicate developmental psychology research effectively
