# PSYC7029 — Practicum I (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7029
- **Title:** Practicum I
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** First supervised clinical practicum. Doctoral-level clinical work under supervision. Emphasis on developing competencies in assessment and intervention with increasing complexity.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7024
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** First clinical contact at doctoral level; expectations higher than MSocSc

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply clinical skills at doctoral level
2. Conduct comprehensive clinical interviews
3. Administer and interpret complex psychological assessments
4. Deliver evidence-based interventions with autonomy
5. Engage in doctoral-level supervision (live observation, case conceptualization)
6. Maintain professional documentation
7. Practice within ethical and legal frameworks at doctoral level

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (4-6 hours)
- Doctoral-level expectations
- Site policies
- EHR systems

### Topic 2: Advanced Clinical Interviewing (20-30 hours)
- Doctoral-level interview skills
- Differential diagnostic reasoning
- Cultural formulation at doctoral level
- Research-informed interview approach

### Topic 3: Complex Assessment I (20-30 hours)
- WAIS-IV, MMPI-2-RF
- NEO-PI-R
- Projective (Rorschach CS)
- Integration of multiple data sources

### Topic 4: Intervention Skills I (20-30 hours)
- CBT, psychodynamic
- Evidence-based protocols
- Outcome monitoring
- Adaptations for diverse populations

### Topic 5: Case Formulation (15-20 hours)
- Biopsychosocial formulation
- Cognitive case formulation
- Research-informed case conceptualization
- Linking to literature

### Topic 6: Supervision (weekly)
- Individual and group
- Live observation
- Co-therapy
- Doctoral-level reflection

### Topic 7: Professional Documentation (10-15 hours)
- Comprehensive reports
- Doctoral-level writing
- Research integration
- Communication with referrers

### Topic 8: Cultural Considerations (5-10 hours)
- HK Chinese context
- Cross-cultural competence
- Research on cultural factors

### Topic 9: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 10: Case Conference Presentation (5-8 hours)
- Doctoral-level case presentation
- Integration of research

### Topic 11: Final Evaluation (3 hours)
