# PSYC8012 — Practicum IV (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8012
- **Title:** Practicum IV
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Fourth supervised clinical practicum. Doctoral-level autonomy, full evidence-based protocols, supervision of junior trainees, and preparation for PSU.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC8011
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level pre-PSU

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Deliver doctoral-level full evidence-based protocols
2. Manage treatment-resistant cases with sophistication
3. Apply advanced specialty skills
4. Demonstrate near-doctoral-level competency
5. Supervise and mentor junior trainees
6. Engage in doctoral-level supervision
7. Plan for PSU

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (if new site)

### Topic 2: Full Protocol Implementation (50-70 hours)
- Doctoral-level CBT for depression
- CBT for anxiety
- Trauma-focused CBT
- Other manualized treatments

### Topic 3: Doctoral-Level Advanced Cases (50-70 hours)
- Treatment-resistant
- Comorbid
- Complex trauma
- Personality disorder

### Topic 4: Group Interventions (15-25 hours)
- Group CBT
- Skills groups (DBT)
- Support groups

### Topic 5: Doctoral-Level Consultation and Liaison (10-15 hours)
- Multidisciplinary teams
- Consultation to other professionals
- Crisis consultation

### Topic 6: Supervision (weekly)
- Decreasing supervision
- Doctoral-level autonomy
- Specialty supervision

### Topic 7: Doctoral-Level Mentoring (5-10 hours)
- Doctoral-level peer mentoring
- Co-leading groups
- Sharing clinical knowledge

### Topic 8: Outcome and Quality (5-8 hours)
- Doctoral-level outcome monitoring
- Quality assurance
- Program development input

### Topic 9: Professional Identity (5-8 hours)
- Doctoral-level identity
- Career planning
- Networking

### Topic 10: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 11: Case Conference Presentation (8-10 hours)

### Topic 12: Evaluation (3 hours)
