# PSYC7030 — Practicum II (PhD Clinical Psychology)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7030
- **Title:** Practicum II
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Second supervised clinical practicum. Increased complexity, autonomy, and case variety. Doctoral-level integration of research and practice.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7029
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level autonomy; research-practice integration

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Manage a more complex doctoral-level caseload
2. Apply advanced clinical skills with autonomy
3. Integrate research and practice in case conceptualization
4. Conduct comprehensive assessments
5. Deliver evidence-based interventions independently
6. Demonstrate professional development
7. Engage in advanced supervision

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Practicum Site Orientation (3-4 hours, if new site)

### Topic 2: Complex Case Management (40-60 hours)
- Doctoral-level case complexity
- Comorbidities
- Treatment-resistant cases
- Severe presentations

### Topic 3: Comprehensive Assessment (20-30 hours)
- Full battery administration
- Integration of multiple data sources
- Differential diagnosis at doctoral level
- Research-informed reports

### Topic 4: Intervention Skills II (40-60 hours)
- Full evidence-based protocols
- Adaptations for diverse populations
- Outcome monitoring and feedback
- Research-practice integration

### Topic 5: Case Formulation II (15-25 hours)
- Advanced biopsychosocial formulation
- Research-informed case conceptualization
- Maintenance and relapse prevention
- Termination planning

### Topic 6: Supervision (weekly)
- Increased depth
- Live observation
- Co-therapy
- Doctoral-level reflection

### Topic 7: Cultural Considerations (5-10 hours)
- HK Chinese context
- Cross-cultural competence
- Research on cultural factors

### Topic 8: Consultation (5-10 hours)
- Within team
- With other professionals
- With families

### Topic 9: Log of Hours (ongoing)

### Topic 10: Case Conference Presentation (5-8 hours)
- Doctoral-level presentation
- Research-practice integration

### Topic 11: Final Evaluation (3 hours)
