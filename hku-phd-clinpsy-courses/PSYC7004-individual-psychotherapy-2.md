# PSYC7004 — Individual Psychotherapy II (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7004
- **Title:** Individual psychotherapy II
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level continuation of Individual Psychotherapy I. Advanced skills in CBT and psychodynamic therapy, integration of techniques, application to specific disorders, and management of complex therapeutic challenges. Research-informed approach.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7003
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level advanced psychotherapy

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply doctoral-level advanced CBT techniques
2. Apply doctoral-level advanced psychodynamic interventions
3. Integrate techniques across orientations with sophistication
4. Address resistance and ruptures at doctoral level
5. Manage dropout risk
6. Adapt psychotherapy to specific disorders with research-informed approach
7. Evaluate therapeutic outcomes rigorously
8. Conduct psychotherapy research

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Advanced CBT (8 hours)
- Doctoral-level schema-focused CBT
- Behavioral experiments
- Exposure therapy
- Cognitive restructuring for personality
- Mindfulness-based CBT
- Research evidence

### Topic 2: Advanced Psychodynamic (6 hours)
- Object relations interventions
- TFP
- MBT
- Short-term dynamic therapies
- Interpretive work
- Research evidence

### Topic 3: Integrative Psychotherapy (6 hours)
- Doctoral-level technical eclecticism
- Transtheoretical model
- Common factors + specific techniques
- Case-based integration
- Research on integration

### Topic 4: Working with Resistance (5 hours)
- Doctoral-level recognition
- Therapeutic strategies
- Meta-communication
- Maintaining alliance

### Topic 5: Managing Ruptures (5 hours)
- Rupture types
- Repair strategies
- Alliance-focused therapy
- Negotiation
- Research on ruptures

### Topic 6: Dropout Prevention (3 hours)
- Doctoral-level risk factors
- Engagement strategies
- Premature termination
- Outreach

### Topic 7: Disorder-Specific Applications (8 hours)
- Doctoral-level depression treatment
- Anxiety disorders
- OCD
- PTSD
- Personality disorders
- Substance use

### Topic 8: Outcome Monitoring (3 hours)
- Session-by-session measures
- Feedback-informed treatment
- Doctoral-level progress assessment
- Stepped care

### Topic 9: Therapist Self-Reflection (3 hours)
- Doctoral-level therapist factors
- Personal therapy
- Self-care
- Professional development

### Topic 10: Recorded Session Review (3 hours)
- Doctoral-level peer and supervisor feedback
- Self-assessment
- Goal setting

### Topic 11: Psychotherapy Research (4 hours)
- Efficacy vs. effectiveness
- Mechanism research
- Process-outcome research
- Practice-based evidence
