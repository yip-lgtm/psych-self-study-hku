# PSYC8001 — Clinical Seminar II (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8001
- **Title:** Clinical seminar II
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level advanced case-based seminar. Complex cases, integration of multiple theoretical orientations, evidence-based practice, professional issues, and preparation for entry-level doctoral practice.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7009
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level case conference

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Present complex cases with doctoral-level sophistication
2. Apply doctoral-level integrative case conceptualization
3. Critically evaluate evidence base at doctoral level
4. Address complex professional and ethical issues
5. Demonstrate doctoral-level clinical leadership
6. Prepare for entry-level doctoral practice
7. Integrate research and practice at doctoral level

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Doctoral-Level Advanced Case Presentation (3 hours)
- Complex multi-problem cases
- Integration across orientations
- Cultural and systemic factors
- Research integration

### Topic 2: Integrative Case Conceptualization (6 hours)
- Doctoral-level multi-orientation formulation
- Sophisticated intervention selection
- Sequencing and pacing
- Research evidence

### Topic 3: Evidence-Based Practice (6 hours)
- Doctoral-level EBP tripartite model
- Critically appraising literature
- Doctoral-level application
- Stepped care
- Practice-based evidence

### Topic 4: Complex Cases — Severe Mental Illness (5 hours)
- Doctoral-level schizophrenia
- Bipolar disorder
- Treatment-resistant depression
- Risk management
- Recovery-oriented practice

### Topic 5: Complex Cases — Personality Disorders (5 hours)
- Doctoral-level BPD
- Narcissistic PD
- Treatment planning
- Therapy relationship challenges
- Research on PD treatment

### Topic 6: Complex Cases — Trauma (4 hours)
- Doctoral-level complex PTSD
- Dissociative disorders
- Phase-oriented treatment
- Research on trauma treatment

### Topic 7: Cultural and Diversity Issues (3 hours)
- HK Chinese context
- Cross-cultural work
- Linguistic considerations
- Refugee and migrant populations
- Indigenous psychologies

### Topic 8: Professional and Ethical Issues (4 hours)
- Doctoral-level complex dilemmas
- Boundary issues
- Confidentiality limits
- Risk management
- Professional communication
- Research ethics

### Topic 9: Doctoral-Level Clinical Leadership (3 hours)
- Leading teams
- Doctoral-level consultation
- Program development
- Supervision of junior trainees

### Topic 10: Preparation for Entry-Level Doctoral Practice (4 hours)
- Doctoral-level licensure (HKPS DCP)
- Career planning
- Continuing professional development
- Networking

### Topic 11: Final Portfolio Presentation (5 hours)
- Doctoral-level comprehensive portfolio
- Self-evaluation
- Program reflection

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Integrative | Combining multiple approaches |
| EBP | Evidence-based practice |
| Stepped care | Treatment intensity matched to need |
| Phase-oriented | Sequenced treatment phases |
| Complex PTSD | Result of prolonged trauma |

| Risk management | Strategies to mitigate risk |

| Leadership | Influencing others toward goals |
| Licensure | Regulatory authorization |
