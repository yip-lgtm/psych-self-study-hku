# PSYC8005 — Ethics and Professional Issues (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8005
- **Title:** Ethics and professional issues
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1 or 2 (flexible)
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level ethical principles, professional standards, and legal framework. Advanced ethical reasoning, complex dilemmas, research ethics, and professional development at doctoral level.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7024
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level comprehensive ethics

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Apply doctoral-level HKPS Code
2. Apply doctoral-level APA Ethics
3. Navigate HK legal framework with sophistication
4. Address doctoral-level complex ethical dilemmas
5. Practice within doctoral-level scope of competence
6. Manage doctoral-level professional relationships
7. Develop doctoral-level professional identity
8. Conduct research with doctoral-level ethical rigor
