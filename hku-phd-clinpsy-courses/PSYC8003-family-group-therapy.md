# PSYC8003 — Family and Group Therapy (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC8003
- **Title:** Family and group therapy
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 2
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level theory and practice of family and group therapy. Major models, evidence base, advanced application, and research integration.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7004
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level family/group

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Describe major family therapy models with research literacy
2. Apply doctoral-level systemic formulation
3. Conduct family therapy interventions with sophistication
4. Describe major group therapy models and evidence
5. Lead group therapy interventions at doctoral level
6. Discuss evidence base critically
7. Adapt therapy to family/group context with cultural competence
8. Conduct research in family/group therapy

## Topic Outline (PhD-level)

### Topic 1: Doctoral-Level Family Therapy Foundations (4 hours)
- History
- Systems theory advanced
- Family life cycle
- HK Chinese family context
- Research

### Topic 2: Bowen Family Systems (5 hours)
- Differentiation of self
- Triangles
- Multigenerational transmission
- Family diagrams
- Research on Bowen

### Topic 3: Structural Family Therapy (5 hours)
- Minuchin's model
- Boundaries and subsystems
- Enactment
- Joining and restructuring
- Research on structural

### Topic 4: Strategic Family Therapy (4 hours)
- Problem-focused approach
- Directives
- Paradoxical interventions
- Research

### Topic 5: Other Family Therapy Models (6 hours)
- SFBT
- Narrative therapy
- EFT
- Milan systemic
- Attachment-based
- Research on each

### Topic 6: Doctoral-Level Systemic Formulation (5 hours)
- Genograms
- Circular questioning
- Hypothesizing
- Neutrality
- Research integration

### Topic 7: Doctoral-Level Group Therapy Foundations (5 hours)
- Curative factors (Yalom)
- Group development stages
- Group composition
- Pre-group preparation
- Research

### Topic 8: Group Therapy Models (5 hours)
- CBT groups
- Interpersonal groups
- Psychodynamic groups
- DBT skills groups
- Support groups
- Research

### Topic 9: Doctoral-Level Group Leadership (5 hours)

- Research

### Topic 10: Cultural and Ethical Issues (3 hours)
