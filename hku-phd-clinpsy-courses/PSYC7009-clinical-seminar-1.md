# PSYC7009 — Clinical Seminar I (PhD)

## Course Info (from HKU Catalog)

- **Code:** PSYC7009
- **Title:** Clinical seminar I
- **Program:** PhD Clinical Psychology (4-year)
- **Year:** 1
- **HKU Description:** Doctoral-level case-based seminar. Advanced case presentation, biopsychosocial formulation, evidence-based treatment planning. Doctoral-level clinical reasoning and research integration.
- **Prerequisites:** PSYC7029
- **Assessment:** 100% coursework
- **Notes:** Doctoral-level case conference

## Learning Objectives (PhD-level)

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

1. Present cases with doctoral-level sophistication
2. Apply doctoral-level biopsychosocial formulation
3. Develop research-informed treatment plans
4. Integrate theory, research, and practice at doctoral level
5. Provide doctoral-level peer feedback
6. Discuss ethical and professional issues at doctoral level
7. Apply DSM-5 and RDoC diagnostic reasoning
