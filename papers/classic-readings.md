# Classic Readings Log

> Log every primary research paper you read. Use the 3-pass method (see `reading-method-3pass.md`).

## Format

```
## [Paper citation in APA 7th]
- **Date read:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Pass 1 (5 min):** [Category, context, take-away]
- **Pass 2 (30 min):** [Method summary, key results]
- **Pass 3 (60 min):** [Reconstructed argument, limitations, your critique]
- **Anki card added?** Y/N
- **Relevance to capstone:** [How does it connect?]
```
